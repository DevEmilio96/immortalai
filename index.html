Our Vision

IMMORTAL AI uses autonomous, reusable, and persistent modules to ensure that learned skills are never lost. This approach allows for infinite scalability and represents a concrete step toward real AGI (Artificial General Intelligence).

The primary goal of IMMORTAL AI is to create an architecture that:

  • Allows continuous learning without loss of skills
  • Modularizes intelligence into autonomous and reusable units
  • Enables decentralized and unlimited growth
  • Prepares the ground for the emergence of general intelligence (AGI)
